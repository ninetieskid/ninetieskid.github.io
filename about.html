About Us

Our passion for Czech wines began with a love for the rich tradition and exceptional quality of Moravian viticulture.

Our Story

Moravia Harvest Imports was founded with a simple mission: to bring the exceptional wines of the Czech Republic's Moravian region to Canadian wine enthusiasts. Our journey began with a deep appreciation for the centuries-old winemaking traditions that have flourished in the rolling hills of South Moravia.

The Czech Republic has a winemaking history dating back over 1,000 years, with the Moravian region producing some of the most distinctive and high-quality wines in Central Europe. These wines reflect the unique terroir of the region, combining continental climate conditions with mineral-rich soils to create wines of exceptional character and complexity.

As a fully licensed alcohol importer in Canada, we work directly with family-owned wineries and established producers throughout Moravia to ensure that every bottle meets our exacting standards for quality and authenticity.

The Moravian Region

Located in the southeastern part of the Czech Republic, Moravia is the country's premier wine region. The area benefits from a warm continental climate, long growing seasons, and diverse soil types that create ideal conditions for viticulture.

From the mineral-driven whites of the Znojmo region to the elegant reds of the Mikulov area, Moravian wines offer incredible diversity and represent some of the best expressions of Central European winemaking.
